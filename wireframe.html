LAYOUT PRINCIPLES

  • Alternating dark (black) and light (cream) sections for visual rhythm
  • Gold accents used sparingly — as dividers, headlines, CTAs
  • Serif display fonts for elegance, sans-serif only for navigation
  • Generous white space — luxury comes from what's NOT on the page
  • Mobile-first responsive grid (1 column on mobile → 4 columns on desktop)
  • Hover animations on cards · Fade-in on scroll · Autoplay muted videos
